NodeGirls Xmas @Sydney

Where and when:

19th of November 2016 at CoderFactory Academy, Level 2, 7 Kelly str., Ultimo, 2007, NSW

What it's all about:

We believe that any woman should try programming at least once in their life. There for we created a community where in friendly atmosphere with great vibe you can try programming first time or if you already code - learn something new about JavaScript and/or Node.js.

Our half day coding bootcamps is also a great opportunity to grow your network and meet new people with similar interests. Come and join our FREE event for women with lunch provided.

What to bring with you:

  • - laptop (if you do not have laptop write us directly - we will figure something out for you)
  • - charger
  • - desire to learn
  • - good mood

Agenda of the day:

  • 9:45 - 10:00 Registration
  • 10:00 - 10:30 Intro + Group formation
  • 10:30 - 12:00 Coding workshop with help of mentors
  • 12:00 - 12:45 Lunch, generously provided by TabCorp
  • 12:45 - 2:30 Coding workshop with help of mentors
  • 2:45 - 3:00 Tea and sweets, generously provided by Hired
  • 3:00 - 3:10 Wrap up, next steps

On the day attendees will be divided on 3 groups by their skills (complete beginners, intermediate and advanced) with following up short presentations on how to start and following up coding exercises. The format of the event will be code-alone with the explanations in the tasks and help of our mentors.

How much does it cost:

This event is FREE of charge. With lunch and soft drinks provided. It is possible because of our generouse sponsors: CoderFactory Academy, TabCorp and Hired.

How get a ticket:

Simply fill in the REGISTRATION FORM, with few questions about your dietary requirements and level of programming skills, so we can better organize day for you.

If you have any further questions don't hesitate to email us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, would like to become a mentor, sponsor an event or want to be involved in some way, drop us a line to info@nodegirls.com.au.
